Why Privacy Matters

The Problem

Orders, triggers, and strategies are visible on-chain without privacy. This enables frontrunning, copytrading, and price manipulation, especially with microcaps.

The Solution

With RoboLink, your rules remain hidden until the exact moment of execution. Full transparency of results, full privacy of intent.
